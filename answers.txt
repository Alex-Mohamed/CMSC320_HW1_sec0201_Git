Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: While merging can cause one's commits to remain branched off from the main/target branch, rebasing allows these changes to be moved to the end of the main/target branch, no matter what has been added to the end of the main/target branch while others commits were being worked on.
